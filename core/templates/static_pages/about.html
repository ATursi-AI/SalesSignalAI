About SalesSignal AI

Humans backed by AI. AI backed by humans.

Our Mission

SalesSignal AI exists to help local service businesses find customers who need them right now. We believe the best leads aren't on some purchased list — they're hidden in public records, social channels, and community forums. We find them before anyone else does.

What We Do

We monitor 37+ data sources 24/7 to detect real-time buying signals for service businesses. When a building gets a plumbing violation, a restaurant fails a health inspection, or a homeowner posts "I need an electrician" on Nextdoor — we find it, package it as an actionable lead, and deliver it to the right business.

Our platform combines:

  • AI-powered lead detection across public records, social media, review sites, and weather alerts
  • Automated email outreach with personalized multi-touch sequences
  • Human phone follow-up to qualify leads and book appointments
  • Full CRM and sales pipeline to manage every opportunity from detection to close

Who We Serve

We serve 50+ service categories including plumbing, electrical, HVAC, commercial cleaning, roofing, pest control, landscaping, moving, insurance, and general contracting. If people need your service, we can find them.

Andrew Tursi

Founder & CEO

Andrew founded SalesSignal AI in 2025 in New York City with a simple observation: local service businesses were spending thousands on marketing but missing the leads that were hiding in plain sight. By combining public data, AI analysis, and real human follow-up, SalesSignal AI helps service businesses find and close customers they'd never have known about.
